Why Vortex Client?

Combat

Keystrokes Settings

Displays movement keys and clicks on screen.

Combat Log Timer

Tracks the time since your last combat log.

Visual

Armour View Settings

Shows your armor status and durability.

Cps Counter Settings

Displays your clicks per second (CPS).

Ping Counter Settings

Shows your current ping to the server.

Utility

Resolution Adjuster Settings

Change game resolution without restarting.

Notifications

Show alerts for events and key actions.

Cinematic Mode Settings

Smooth camera motion for cinematic use.

Cosmetics

Nametags Settings

Customize the appearance of player nametags.

Custom Cape Settings

Equip and display your personalized cape.

How to Install

Step 1: Install Tampermonkey

Make sure you have the Tampermonkey browser extension installed. Get it here.

Step 2: Install the Userscript

Click the button below to install Vortex Client directly from GitHub.

Step 3: Play!

Navigate to bloxd.io, and the client will automatically activate.
