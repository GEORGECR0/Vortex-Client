Client Features

Armour View

Shows what armor you are wearing without opening inventory.

Keystrokes

Displays your movement keys and clicks on screen.

CPS Counter

Displays your current clicks per second.

Crosshairs

Customize your crosshairs to your liking.

Ping Counter

Shows your current ping to the server you are on.

Resolution Adjuster

Change your game resolution without needing to restart.

Cinematic Mode

It removes the game ui to make your screen cleaner.

Screen Recorder

An in client screen recorder to record your gameplay.

Custom Nametags

Customize the appearance of your nametags.
